Rules of the Game

  • Goal: Be the first player to reach 100 points.
  • On your turn:
    • Roll a six-sided die.
    • If you roll a number from 2 to 6, the number gets added to your turn total.
    • If you roll a 1, your turn ends and your turn total becomes 0.
  • Hold: You can choose to "hold" at any time to add your turn total to your overall score and end your turn.
  • Winning: The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
